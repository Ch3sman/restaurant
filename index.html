About The Butchery

Parts & Labor Your Remington neighborhood butcher shop and hearth-driven restaurant. Visit usin Baltimore! Butcher shop with locally sourced, pasture raised, whole animal butchery, and stocked with hand-picked provisions. Restaurant serving the region’s finest meats from the hearth and 20 craft beers on tap.
